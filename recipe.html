Delicious Recipes

Discover origins, ingredients, and specialties from around the world

Litti Chokha

Origin: Bihar, India

Ingredients: Wheat flour, Sattu, Garlic, Mustard oil, Eggplant, Tomato, Potato

Making Process: Bake stuffed dough balls and serve with mashed smoked vegetables seasoned with mustard oil and spices.

Specialty: Traditional rustic flavor with smoky undertones.

Khajuria

Origin: Bihar, India

Ingredients: Wheat flour, Jaggery, Fennel seeds, Ghee

Making Process: Prepare dough with jaggery and spices, shape into discs and deep fry till golden.

Specialty: Sweet and crunchy festive snack.

Thekua

Origin: Bihar, India

Ingredients: Wheat flour, Jaggery, Coconut, Cardamom, Ghee

Making Process: Mix ingredients into dough, mold and fry until crisp and brown.

Specialty: A traditional offering during Chhath Puja, loved for its crunch and sweetness.

Paneer Butter Masala

Origin: India

Ingredients: Paneer, Tomatoes, Cream, Butter, Spices

Making Process: Cook tomato gravy, add cream and spices, simmer with paneer cubes.

Specialty: Rich and creamy North Indian delicacy.

Dal Pitha

Origin: Bihar, India

Ingredients: Rice flour, Chana dal, Spices, Mustard seeds

Making Process: Fill rice dough with spicy dal paste and steam until soft and cooked through.

Specialty: Healthy and protein-rich traditional steamed snack.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Origin: Italy

Ingredients: Spaghetti, Eggs, Pancetta, Parmesan, Pepper

Making Process: Cook pasta, mix with eggs, pancetta, and cheese off heat for creamy texture.

Specialty: Creamy texture without using cream.

Sushi

Origin: Japan

Ingredients: Sushi rice, Nori, Raw fish, Vegetables, Vinegar

Making Process: Roll seasoned rice and fillings in nori using a bamboo mat, slice and serve.

Specialty: Minimalist preparation with delicate balance of flavors.

Tacos

Origin: Mexico

Ingredients: Tortillas, Meat/Beans, Salsa, Lettuce, Cheese

Making Process: Fill tortillas with desired fillings, top with salsa and cheese.

Specialty: Street food favorite with endless variations.

Croissant

Origin: France

Ingredients: Flour, Butter, Yeast, Sugar, Salt

Making Process: Layer dough with butter, fold and roll repeatedly, bake until golden.

Specialty: Buttery, flaky pastry perfect for breakfast.
